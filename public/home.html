How to Learn DevOps from Scratch

To get started with DevOps, consider following these steps:

  1. Understand the basics of software development and operations.
  2. Familiarize yourself with version control systems like Git.
  3. Learn about continuous integration and continuous deployment practices.
  4. Explore cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud.
  5. Take online courses on platforms like Coursera, Udacity, or Udemy.
  6. Join communities and participate in discussions to stay updated on the latest trends.

Good luck on your DevOps journey!
